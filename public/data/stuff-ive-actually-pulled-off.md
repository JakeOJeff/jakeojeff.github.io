# stuff ive actually pulled off (a lil brag post)

## Date : 15-07-2026

okay i dont usually do the whole "list my achievements" thing because it feels weird, but someone told me i should actually write this stuff down instead of letting it disappear into the git history. so here it is, the stuff im genuinely proud of. and honestly writing it out, its more than i realized

on the code side, the big one is **lat** (LatLang), a real ruby-based compiler that turns my own custom language into lua. lexer, parser, code generator, the whole pipeline, and i shipped it as an actual rubygem. its my most starred repo and the thing that taught me the most about how programming actually works. beyond that ive got **88+ public repos** across lua, typescript, ruby, go, python, java and c, i built the whole **tales of orbis universe** (three games, two engines, playable in the browser), **spellfluid** the fluid simulator, **jengine** my physics engine, **stacks against you** the zero-database chat app, and a unix shell from scratch in c. i also placed **top 5 on a hackclub leaderboard** for my physics engine work which got me a set of rpg trading cards, and i did summer of making with OSSint.

but its not all code and thats kind of the point. im a **bounty hunter for hackclub**, i **moderate the w3schools discord**, i run **Vellapaper** which is a real e-commerce store with real customers, and i founded and ran my own minecraft server **MinersJoK** back in the day. and outside the screen i play **guitar at open gigs** and write my own music and poetry. i dont say all this to flex (okay maybe a little lol), its more that i keep forgetting how much has stacked up, and its a good reminder to myself: just keep building, it adds up way faster than it feels like in the moment :D

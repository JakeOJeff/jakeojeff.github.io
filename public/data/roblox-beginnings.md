# my first ever games were roblox obbies

## Date : ~2019 (i was like 11, cant remember exactly)

okay so way before any of this github stuff, before löve, before literally anything, i was just a kid making obbies on roblox. like those obstacle course games where u jump across platforms and try not to fall. i was OBSESSED. i'd spend hours in roblox studio just placing blocks and setting up jumps and adding those little glitch sections to mess with people lol

i didnt really know what i was doing was "programming" at the time. i was just dragging stuff around and messing with the lua scripts when i needed something to move or open or kill you. but looking back thats where it all started. the fact that i could BUILD a thing that other people could play? that hooked me instantly and it never let go

i think i was around 11? honestly the dates are fuzzy this far back. but yeah. roblox obbies. thats the origin story. not glamorous but its real
